I hate commenting on pull requests. I just hate it.
Telling someone they could have done it different after they just did the work
and treating it as blocking part of the process is a combo I detest.

We've had good success with Martin Folwler's ship-show-ask approach in my current team.
Instead of comments about how it could be improved, I just change it myself after the fact and send a new show PR.
details: https://martinfowler.com/articles/ship-show-ask.html

To push a PR after the work is done is an anti-pattern.
The feedback must start during the work.
